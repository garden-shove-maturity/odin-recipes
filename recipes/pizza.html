Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a round, flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce,

cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked at high heat until

the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and bubbly, pizza offers a perfect balance of chewy dough,

savory toppings, and rich flavor. From classic Margherita with fresh basil and mozzarella to creative

modern variations, pizza is endlessly adaptable and enjoyed worldwide as a comforting, shareable

meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (flour, water, yeast, salt, and oil)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil or oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough - Mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil, then knead and let it rise until doubled in size.
  2. Shape the base - Roll or stretch the dough into a round or rectangular shape on a floured surface.
  3. Add the sauce - Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add toppings - Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your favorite ingredients like pepperoni, mushrooms, or basil.
  5. Bake - Cook in a hot oven (around 475°F / 250°C) until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
